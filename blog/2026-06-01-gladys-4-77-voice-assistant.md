Hey everyone,

A new version of Gladys is available, with several nice improvements, including a brand-new **Voice Assistant** widget you can drop right onto your dashboard 🎙️

{/* truncate */}

## 🎙️ New Voice Assistant widget

You can now add a **Voice Assistant** widget directly to your dashboard.

[![Voice Assistant widget demo](../static/img/articles/gladys-4-77-voice-assistant/youtube.jpg)](https://youtube.com/shorts/7LBeCTegKsY)

Handy on a wall-mounted tablet or a smartphone. I'd love your feedback, it's first and foremost a proof of concept that could grow into more voice features in Gladys if you find it useful!

## 📊 Gauge widget: customizable color thresholds

Thanks to a contribution from [@Terdious](https://community.gladysassistant.com/), the Gauge widget becomes even more flexible. You can now define **custom color thresholds** to instantly see whether a value is in a low, normal, or critical zone.

![Gauge widget color thresholds](../static/img/articles/gladys-4-77-voice-assistant/01.png)

![Gauge widget in action](../static/img/articles/gladys-4-77-voice-assistant/02.png)

Perfect for tracking temperature, humidity, electricity consumption, or any other important indicator.

## 📥 System logs download

Diagnosing a problem just got easier. A new button lets you **easily download the system logs** from the Gladys settings, which makes sharing information much simpler when you ask for help.

![Download system logs](../static/img/articles/gladys-4-77-voice-assistant/03.png)

## 🔌 Tuya integration improvements

The Tuya integration keeps progressing thanks to [@Terdious](https://community.gladysassistant.com/):

- Local support for the Tuya 3.4 protocol
- Local support for the Tuya 3.5 protocol
- Various stability and compatibility improvements

Great news for Tuya users who want to keep local control of their devices.
